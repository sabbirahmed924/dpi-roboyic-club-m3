About DPI Robotic Club

DPI Robotic Club is a student-driven initiative at Dhaka Polytechnic Institute focused on innovation, engineering, and collaboration. We build robots, compete in national challenges, and explore cutting-edge technologies like AI, IoT, and automation.

Our Mission

  • Empower students to build real-world robotics projects
  • Promote teamwork and technical leadership
  • Represent DPI in national and international competitions
